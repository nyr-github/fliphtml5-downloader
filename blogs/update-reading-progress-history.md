# Reading Progress & History Feature

## Introduction

We've introduced a comprehensive reading progress tracking system that automatically saves your position while reading and provides a dedicated history page to manage all your recently read books.

## What's New

- **Automatic Progress Saving**: Your reading position is now automatically saved as you read, so you can seamlessly continue from where you left off
- **Reading History Page**: A new `/history` page displays all your recently read books with visual progress indicators
- **Progress Indicators**: Each book in your history shows a progress bar and percentage, making it easy to see how far you've read
- **Quick Resume**: One-click access to continue reading any book from your history
- **History Management**: Delete individual books or clear your entire reading history with confirmation dialogs
- **Touch Gestures**: Enhanced mobile experience with swipe navigation and touch-friendly progress bar controls

## How to Use

1. **Reading a Book**: Simply open any book and start reading. Your progress is automatically saved in your browser's local storage
2. **Viewing History**: Click on "Reading History" in the navigation menu to see all your recently read books
3. **Resuming Reading**: Click the "Continue" button on any book in your history to jump right back to where you stopped
4. **Managing History**: Use the delete button on individual books or the clear all option to manage your reading history

## Improvements

- Never lose your reading position again
- Easy access to all your recently read books in one place
- Visual progress tracking helps you manage your reading goals
- Intuitive touch controls for mobile and tablet users
- Clean, organized interface for managing your reading library

## Summary

This update transforms the reading experience by adding persistence and organization to your flipbook reading journey. Whether you're reading a few pages or diving deep into a large publication, your progress is always saved and easily accessible. Thank you for using our platform and we hope this feature enhances your reading experience!
