- **Enhanced Q&A Documentation**: Added clear instructions in our FAQ section explaining the URL conversion trick

## How to Use

### Quick URL Conversion

1. Open any FlipHTML5 book in your browser
2. Click on the address bar
3. Change `fliphtml5.com` to `fliphtml5.aivaded.com`
4. Press Enter - you're now on our download page!

**Example:**
- Original: `https://fliphtml5.com/amcii/vsje/The_Galenian_Spring_2026/`
- Modified: `https://fliphtml5.aivaded.com/amcii/vsje/The_Galenian_Spring_2026/`
