## Method 3: Browser Print Function (Manual Method)

**Best for:** Quick single-page saves  
**Price:** Free  
**Difficulty:** Moderate

This method uses your browser's built-in print-to-PDF functionality.

### Steps

1. Open the flipbook in your browser
2. Navigate to each page manually
3. Press `Ctrl+P` (Windows) or `Cmd+P` (Mac)
4. Select "Save as PDF" as the destination
5. Click "Save" and choose your location
6. Repeat for each page

### Drawbacks

- ❌ Very time-consuming for multi-page books
- ❌ Requires manual page turning
- ❌ May miss lazy-loaded content
- ❌ Lower quality than direct downloads
- ❌ No batch processing capability

**Verdict:** Only use this as a last resort for single pages.
