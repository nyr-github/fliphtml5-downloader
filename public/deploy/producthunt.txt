First Comment:
Hey Product Hunt community! 👋

I'm excited to share FlipHTML5 pdf Downloader - a tool I built to solve a real problem I faced.

The Problem: I needed to download FlipHTML5 books for offline reading, but found:
- Browser extensions require manual page-flipping (so tedious for 100+ page books!)
- Screen captures lose quality and miss original resolution
- Commercial services charge monthly subscriptions

The Solution: A free web tool that:
✅ Pastes URL → Gets PDF in seconds
✅ Accesses original high-quality resources, not screen captures
✅ Handles batch downloads with queue management
✅ Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop
✅ No installation or registration required

Technical highlight: It decrypts FlipHTML5's WASM-encrypted configuration to get original page URLs, then assembles them into a properly formatted PDF.

Would love to hear your feedback and use cases! What features would make this more useful for you?
