Name: FlipHTML5 pdf Downloader - Flipbook to PDF Converter
Short Summary: Free online tool to convert FlipHTML5 flipbooks into high-quality PDF files

Full Description:
FlipHTML5 pdf Downloader is an elegant web-based tool that converts FlipHTML5 publications into high-quality PDF files instantly. Simply paste a FlipHTML5 URL and get your PDF download automatically. No software installation required.

Features:
- One-click URL to PDF conversion with original quality
- Batch download support with task queue management
- ZIP-compressed page resource compatibility
- Community discovery of popular publications
- Reading progress tracking with IndexedDB
- Reading history with progress indicators
- Cross-platform: works on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- Free and open source, no registration required
- Concurrent download pool for faster processing
- Automatic metadata extraction (title, author, page count)
